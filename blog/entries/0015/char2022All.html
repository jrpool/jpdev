Jonathan Robert Pool

Tentative accessibility scores for home pages of charities

Introduction

The table below compares the home pages of 74 charities on a preliminary assessment of accessibility. The page names are links to the home pages on the web. The scores are results of a small battery of automated accessibility tests. The ideal score would be 0; the larger the score, the worse the result. The purpose of this testing was to select a subset of charities—those with relatively good scores—for more robust testing.
