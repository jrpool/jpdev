Charitable accessibility, 2022

Jonathan Robert Pool

Accessibility scores for home pages of charities

After my 2021 comparison of 121 charities, I have performed a new accessibility comparison in 2022, in two stages. In the first stage, I used a small set of tests on the home pages of 74 charities. In the second stage, I selected the 38 charities with the best first-stage results and conducted an improved suite of tests on those 38 charities’ home pages.
