Introduction

How accessible is the Foundation for Individual Rights and Expression web page at https://www.thefire.org/?

This digest can help answer that question. Nine different tools (Alfa, ASLint, Axe, Equal Access, HTML CodeSniffer, Nu Html Checker, QualWeb, Testaro, and WAVE) tested the page to check its compliance with their accessibility rules. In all, the tools define about 915 rules, which are classified here into about 300 accessibility issues.

The results were interpreted to yield a score, with 0 being ideal. The score for this page was 4105, the sum of 880 for the count of issues, 2001 for specific issues, 583 for tool-by-tool ratings, 300 for the page preventing tools from running, 334 for browser warnings, and 7 for delayed page responses.
