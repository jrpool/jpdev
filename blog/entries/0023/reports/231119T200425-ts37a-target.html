Introduction

How accessible is the Genocide Watch web page at https://www.genocidewatch.com/?

This digest can help answer that question. Nine different tools (Alfa, ASLint, Axe, Equal Access, HTML CodeSniffer, Nu Html Checker, QualWeb, Testaro, and WAVE) tested the page to check its compliance with their accessibility rules. In all, the tools define about 915 rules, which are classified here into about 300 accessibility issues.

The results were interpreted to yield a score, with 0 being ideal. The score for this page was 10606, the sum of 740 for the count of issues, 7709 for specific issues, 1888 for tool-by-tool ratings, 0 for the page preventing tools from running, 259 for browser warnings, and 10 for delayed page responses.
