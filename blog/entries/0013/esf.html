Accessibility of web pages related to Esperantic Studies Foundation

Jonathan Pool

Introduction

The following table reports the results of executing the p03c10 accessibility test battery of the Autotest application on web pages of some of organizations and projects related to the Esperantic Studies Foundation. A deficit score of 0 indicates that a page has passed all the tests. Any deficit score greater than 0 indicates that at least one test has found at least one fault on the page.

The tests include three external packages of tests: Axe, IBM Accessibility Checker, and WAVE. In addition, there are ten custom accessibility tests.

The tests were conducted on 18 September 2021.
