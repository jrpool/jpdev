Accessibility of web pages of major United States pharmacy companies

Jonathan Pool

Introduction

The following table reports the results of executing the p03c10 accessibility test battery of the Autotest application on web pages of some of the major companies in the pharmacy industry in the United States. A deficit score of 0 indicates that a page has passed all the tests. Any deficit score greater than 0 indicates that at least one test has found at least one fault on the page.

The tests include three external packages of tests: Axe, IBM Accessibility Checker, and WAVE. In addition, there are ten custom accessibility tests.

The tests were conducted on 17 September 2021.
