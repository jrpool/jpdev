Accessibility of main web pages of CVS Health and selected peers

Jonathan Pool

Introduction

The following table reports the results of executing version 7 of the a11y accessibility test battery of the Autotest application on selected web pages of CVS Health and some of its peers. A deficit score of 0 indicates that a page has passed all the tests. Any deficit score greater than 0 indicates that at least one test has found at least one fault on the page.

The tests include three external packages of tests: Axe, IBM Accessibility Checker, and WAVE. In addition, there are 16 custom accessibility tests.

The tests were conducted on 15 December 2021.
