Accessibility of VEIGX web pages

Jonathan Pool

Introduction

The following table reports the results of executing the p03c11 accessibility test battery of the Autotest application on web pages of the companies in the Vanguard Global ESG Select Stock Fund (ticker symbol VEIGX). Vanguard states that its choice of companies for this fund gives greater exposure to companies with attractive environmental, social, and governance characteristics.

A deficit score of 0 indicates that a page has passed all the tests. Any deficit score greater than 0 indicates that at least one test has found at least one fault on the page.

The tests include three external packages of tests: Axe, IBM Accessibility Checker, and WAVE. In addition, there are eleven custom accessibility tests defined in Autotest.

The tests were conducted on 26 September 2021.
