The procedure performed 1230 tests. Of these, 24 are custom tests defined by Testaro, and the others belong to these seven other packages (programs that perform collections of tests):

  • Alfa by Siteimprove
  • Axe-core by Deque
  • Continuum by Level Access
  • HTML CodeSniffer by Squiz Labs
  • Equal Access by IBM
  • Nu Html Checker
  • Tenon by Level Access
