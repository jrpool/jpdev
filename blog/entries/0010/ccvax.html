Accessibility of CCVAX web pages

Jonathan Pool

Introduction

The following table reports the results of executing the p03c11 accessibility test battery of the Autotest application on web pages of the companies in the Calvert Small-Cap Fund (ticker symbol CCVAX). Calvert states that its choice of companies for this fund is based in part on a disciplined analysis of environmental, social, and governance (ESG) factors.

A deficit score of 0 indicates that a page has passed all the tests. Any deficit score greater than 0 indicates that at least one test has found at least one fault on the page.

The tests include three external packages of tests: Axe, IBM Accessibility Checker, and WAVE. In addition, there are eleven custom accessibility tests defined in Autotest.

The tests were conducted from 20 to 25 September 2021.
