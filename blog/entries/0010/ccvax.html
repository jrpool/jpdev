Accessibility of web pages of socially responsible companies

Jonathan Pool

Introduction

The following table reports the results of executing the p03c10 accessibility test battery of the Autotest application on web pages of the companies in the Calvert Small-Cap Fund (ticker symbol CCVAX). Calvert states that its choice of companies for this fund is based in part on a disciplined analysis of environmental, social, and governance (ESG) factors.

A deficit score of 0 indicates that a page has passed all the tests. Any deficit score greater than 0 indicates that at least one test has found at least one fault on the page.

The tests include three external packages of tests: Axe, IBM Accessibility Checker, and WAVE. In addition, there are ten custom accessibility tests.

The tests were conducted on 20 September 2021.
