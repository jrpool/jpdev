Hypothesis 1: People Can Perform Minimal Disambiguation Tasks

Agreement

On average, 74% of all (1646) choices were in the sentence-task majority.

Consistency

On average, two-task subjects were consistent in 77% of all (485) trials.

Duration

On average, trials lasted:

  • 22 seconds if one-task.
  • 36 seconds if two-task.

Subjective Ease

On average, subjects rated the study's ease at 2.4 on a 0-4 scale.
