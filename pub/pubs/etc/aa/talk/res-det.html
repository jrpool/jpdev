Question: How Can People Disambiguate Best?

Two Tasks: More Agreement?

No. Majority choices among subjects performing

  • One task: 75%.
  • Two tasks: 73%.

Task Type: Affects Agreement?

Not much.

Majority choices by task:

  • Paraphrasal: 76%.
  • Truth-conditional: 72%.

Agreement between subject pairs by task:

  • Both paraphrasal: 68%.
  • Both truth-conditional: 66%.
