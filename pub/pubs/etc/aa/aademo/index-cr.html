Participate in a Study on Meaning!

What?

This is a study of a method for clarifying meanings. It may help people make themselves understood to computers and, through them, to other people worldwide, across language barriers. I'm studying how well the method works, so I want some people to try it.

Who?

You are eligible to participate if you can read and write English.

Where?

I am conducting the study on the Web. You can participate anywhere, using a Web browser.

When?

Now! The study will end on Monday, 12 March 2007, at 15:00 (3:00 p.m.), Pacific Daylight Time (22:00 UTC).

How long?

I estimate your participation will take 30 minutes. When you click on "Next" below, you will be allowed 90 minutes to finish.

What do I get?

Participants are unpaid volunteers. By participating, you can contribute to improvements in human-human and human-computer understanding. Also, you can get a report showing your results in the study, compared with the results of all participants.

How?

I have prepared a Participation Agreement. If you accept it, you can begin.
