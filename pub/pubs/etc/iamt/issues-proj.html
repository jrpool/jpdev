Purpose: Build a tool or a metatool?

There is no commonly accepted theory of interlingua, and the problem is too big to address from scratch in the life span of a typical research or development effort. ... However, what is both necessary and feasible in the near term is the development of a methodology for building interlingual representations. Such a methodology would involve interlingua theory, representation design, encoding/annotation manuals, actual text encoding/annotation, and evaluation measures. IAMTC 2004, "Interlingua"
