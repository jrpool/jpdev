2. Decide whether to re-use

Do ontologies of ambiguity exist?

There are partial classifications of ambiguity and discussions of a possible ontology of ambiguity, but apparently none exists.

We might expect it in GOLD:

GOLD ... gives a formalized account of the most basic categories and relations (the "atoms") used in the scientific description of human language. GOLD Community Website

GOLD might deal with ambiguity in LinguisticFeature:SemanticFeature or SemanticUnit, but not yet.
