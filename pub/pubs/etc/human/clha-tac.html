The Panlingual Lexicon (PanLex)

Conceptual design: FAQ as a specification paradigm.

  • Why FAQ? Because it's modular, user-oriented, and auto-adversarial.
  • Make FAQ read like a manual, and also a reference source.
  • First feasibility test: If you can't explain it, it isn't feasible.
  • Second feasibility test: If you can't believe it, it isn't feasible.
  • Third feasibility test: If your friends don't understand it, it isn't feasible.
  • Fourth feasibility test: If it contains circular dependencies, it may be infeasible.
