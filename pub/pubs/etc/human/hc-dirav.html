What About Directionality?

Same problem with asymmetric verification:

  • Describer seeks to maximize p(Word | Description).
  • Correct facts about Word maximize p(Description | Word).

Example: "astronauts".

  1. Describer might say: "Set foot on the moon".
  2. Describer would not say: "Passed stringent mental-health examinations".

And yet #1 is not a fact about all astronauts, but #2 is.

Do Verbosity's templates eliminate this problem? Probably not. E.g., automobiles contain matter, but would people think to say so?
