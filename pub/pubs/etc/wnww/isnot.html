What's a WordNet not?

Dictionary: No prepositions, conjunctions, complementizers, pronouns.

Thesaurus: " ... not merely an on-line thesaurus" (Miller et al., 1993, p. 3)? Or not even an on-line thesaurus (no major subsumption categories) (Wilks, 2003, p. 15)?

Ontology: (1) WordNet mixes types of conceptual structures; (2) unlike WordNet, "An ontology ... claims its symbols are not words but interlingual or language-free concept names and unique" (Wilks, 2003, pp. 15-16).

Tests: wordless meanings, meaningless words
