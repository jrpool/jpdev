Additional Notes on the Literature of Genocide--Pool

Analytical Categories

1. Definitions (What is genocide?)

2. Causes (Why genocide?)

3. Mechanisms (How does genocide work?)

French, A Continent for the Taking, suggests an explanation for the success of genocide on pp. 141-143.

4. Effects (What does genocide do?)

French, A Continent for the Taking, says:

  • Tutsis massacred Hutus in Rwanda and thereby motivated surviving Hutus to massacre Tutsis.
  • The slave trade eliminated about 2/7 of the population of Africa (p. 22).

5. Solutions (What shall we do about genocide?)
